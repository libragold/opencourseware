---
title: "Calculus Potluck I"
---

Due to the lack of practice exams, Calculus Potluck I, a project conducted by students in section C and H of 21-259 Calculus in Three Dimensions, is launched on October 9. Our goal is to collectively make a practice exam to help people prepare for Exam 2. The editorial board consists Ashwin Raghavachari, Isaac Manjarres, Vivek Nangia and Zilin Jiang (teaching assistant). Special thanks to Lina Pulgarin-Duque, Shana Singh and Sharon Romero who also contributed to this project.
