Common mistakes:

  * Problem 2.4: Many students negate $a>b$ into $a<b$, instead of $a\leq b$.
  * Problem 2.10: Especially for (f), (g) and (h), many students forgot to put &#8216;might&#8217; in the negations. For instance, one simply negate &#8216;Haste makes waste&#8217; as &#8216;Haste does not make waste&#8217;.
