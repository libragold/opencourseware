# Differential and Integral Calculus 1m

Instructor: [Prof. Rom Pinchasi][1], Amado 607, room@math.technion.ac.il

Teaching assistant: Zilin Jiang, Amado 604, jiangzilin@technion.ac.il

Office hour for Zilin Jiang in Amado 604: Sunday 3:30PM – 4:30PM.

Recitations:

  * Recitation 1: Binomial theorem, Pascal (Yanghui) triangle, 1-1, onto and bounded functions, graphs of trig functions, trig formulas. March 23, 20117.
  * Recitation 2: Limit of function, trig functions have limits, limits do not compose, [topologist&#8217;s sine curve][4], what happens when a function with 0 as its limit is multiplied by a bounded function? March 30, 2017.
  * Recitation 3: Application of sandwich theorem and pizza rule, supremum. April 6, 2017.
  * Recitation 4: Monotone increasing function has limit, convergent sequence is bounded, convergence of sequence is insensitive to finite changes, limit of $n^{1/n}$ is 1. April 20, 2017.
  * Recitation 5: [Harmonic series][8], pass limit to exponential, $\lim_{n\to\infty}(1+1/n)^n = e$. April 27, 2017.
  * Recitation 6: $e$ as limit of a function, application of the [intermediate value theorem][10]. May 4, 2017.
  * Recitation 7: Application of [Bolzano–Weirstrass theorem][12], one-to-one continuous function is monotone, derivative can be discontinuous. May 11, 2017.
  * Recitation 8: Application of Heine&#8217;s theorem for non-existence of limit, find maximum and minimum by derivative, application of mean value theorem. May 17, 2017.
  * [Recitation 9](/2017-spring-104018/recitation-9): Application of [l&#8217;Hôpital&#8217;s rule][15] and [Taylor&#8217;s theorem][16]. May 25, 2017.
  * [Recitation 10](/2017-spring-104018/recitation-10): Integration by substitution, integration by parts. June 1, 2017.
  * Recitation 11: One more reduction formula, application of fundamental theorem of calculus, integration by substitution and integration by parts for definite integral, Newton–Leibniz formula, [Euler substitution][19]. June 5, 2017 and June 8, 2017.
  * Recitation 12: Improper integral and its convergence. June 15, 2017.
