title: "Assignment O"
---

There are various ways to get the [disjunctive normal form][1] and the [conjunctive normal form][2] of a given formula. In this assignment, students are asked to calculate the DNF and the CNF of two formulae.

Common Mistakes:

* Some students tried to deduce the CNF and DNF via truth table which involves some mistakes while calculating the truth value.
* Few students were wrong about the definition of CNF and DNF.

[1]: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Disjunctive_normal_form
[2]: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conjunctive_normal_form
